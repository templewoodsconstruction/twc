Project Process

6. Can you explain the steps involved in starting a project with your company?
Our process starts with an initial consultation, followed by design development, budget planning, permit acquisition, and finally, construction.

Yes, we handle all permits and approvals on your behalf, ensuring compliance with local regulations.
8. On average, how long does it take to complete a home extension or new construction project?
